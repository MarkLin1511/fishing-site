Honest note

The fish list is practical, not endless.

You asked for every cool fish around LA. I focused the site on the main freshwater fish beginners are likely to target, hear about, or find in the LA River and nearby local waters.

Best habit

Double-check the live source before a trip.

Regulations, advisories, closures, fees, and product prices can all change after today. The official pages above should be your final check.
