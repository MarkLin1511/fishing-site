What “current” means here

These cards are a time-stamped fishing snapshot for late March 2026. They combine official species lists, current access notes, and the time of year.

Why some cards say shoreline-only

Pyramid Lake had a current DWR update on January 29, 2026 saying recreational boat activities were closed because of water levels, while shoreline and park access remained open.
